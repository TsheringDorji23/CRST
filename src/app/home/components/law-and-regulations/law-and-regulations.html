Know Your Rights

In secured lending, laws provide legal certainty by ensuring that your property rights are recognized, respected, and legally enforceable when assets are pledged as collateral. Understanding these laws helps you make informed financial decisions and confidently protect your assets.

iCoMS is supported by a strong legal framework that protects both lenders and borrowers in every secured transaction.

Access and download all relevant laws and regulations that govern movable and immovable collateral and ensure your rights are safeguarded.
