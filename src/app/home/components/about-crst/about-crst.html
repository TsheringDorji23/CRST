Integrated Collateral Operations & Management System (iCoMS)

iCoMS is a comprehensive, national digital platform developed to streamline the registration, management, and public disclosure of security interests in both movable and immovable property. iCoMS provides a unified and secure system that enhances transparency, reduces risk, and facilitates access to credit through secured transactions. This platform enables borrowers to use a broader range of assets as collateral, while offering lenders a reliable tool to verify existing security interests in real time. The system also ensures public access to accurate and up-to-date information, thereby fostering trust in the credit market and minimizing the risk of fraud or multiple claims on the same asset.

Our Institutional Partnership

  • iCoMS is a joint initiative of the Royal Monetary Authority of Bhutan (RMA) and the National Land Commission Secretariat (NLCS).
  • This collaboration integrates two critical national systems into a single digital platform:
    • Central Registry for Secured Transactions (CRST): Records security interests in movable property and is administered solely by RMA in accordance with the Executive Order issued by the Ministry of Finance on 22 August 2012.
    • Land Mortgage System (LMS): Jointly managed by RMA and NLCS, where NLCS serves as the lead agency responsible for maintaining mortgage records related to immovable property, as mandated by Section 181 of the Land Act 2007. RMA provides overall system oversight and ensures regulatory compliance.
  • By integrating these systems, iCoMS simplifies collateral registration and access, making secured lending faster, more transparent, and more reliable for all stakeholders.
