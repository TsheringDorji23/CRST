Mission

“To strengthen Bhutan’s secured lending ecosystem through a unified, digital platform that ensures transparency, trust, and accessibility in the registration and management of all forms of collateral.”

Vision

“To become a trusted national gateway for secured transactions by enabling real-time visibility of security interests, promoting financial inclusion, and making access to credit easier, safer, and more equitable for all.”

Core
Objectives

  • To combine movable and immovable property records into a single digital platform.
  • To help borrowers use a wider range of assets to secure loans.
  • To provide lenders with transparent, real-time access to verify whether assets are already pledged.
  • To establish and protect the priority of claims, ensuring the legal rights of both lenders and borrowers are upheld.

Key
Functions

  • Register and manage security interests in both movable and immovable property in a secure digital environment.
  • Provide public search access to help users obtain verified information on pledged assets.
  • Support conflict resolution by identifying and displaying the order of registered claims on the same property.
  • Enable digital transactions with features like online payments, real-time updates, and simplified user workflows.
  • Maintain data integrity to ensure accuracy, reduce fraud, and build confidence in the secured lending process.
